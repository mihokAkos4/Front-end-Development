Our Story

Little Lemon is a cherished family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, founded by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian. With a passion for authentic flavors and a vision to bring a modern twist to traditional recipes, Mario and Adrian have created a dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of the Mediterranean.

Our Location

Nestled in the heart of Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a vibrant culinary gem that brings the flavors of the Mediterranean to the Midwest.

Our Cuisine

Drawing inspiration from Italy, Greece, and Turkey, our menu features 12–15 seasonal dishes crafted with the freshest ingredients.

Our Atmosphere

Step into Little Lemon and experience the perfect blend of rustic charm and modern elegance.
